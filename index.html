About

The United States Air Force Band of the West is the Air Force's premier musical organization serving the southern United States. Based at Joint Base San Antonio–Lackland, Texas, its professional Airmen-musicians represent the United States Air Force through performances that honor military service, strengthen community relationships, and inspire future generations.

Each year, the Band of the West presents hundreds of performances throughout Texas, Oklahoma, Louisiana, Mississippi, Alabama, Georgia, Florida, Puerto Rico, and beyond. The organization includes the Concert Band, Ceremonial Marching Band, Dimensions in Blue, Top Flight, Velocity, and several chamber ensembles, allowing the band to perform music ranging from classical masterworks and military ceremonies to jazz and popular music.

Tracing its lineage to 1941, the Band of the West has earned a national reputation for musical excellence while serving audiences at military ceremonies, civic events, schools, and concert halls throughout the region.

Captain David Neil C. Regner

Commander & Conductor

▾

Captain David Neil C. Regner serves as Commander and Conductor of the United States Air Force Band of the West at Joint Base San Antonio–Lackland, Texas. He leads more than 60 professional Airmen-musicians who support military ceremonies, community outreach, recruiting initiatives, and public diplomacy throughout the southern United States.

Prior to assuming command in 2025, Capt Regner served in multiple leadership and conducting assignments within the Air Force Bands program. He holds degrees in music education and conducting and has performed and conducted throughout the United States and internationally.

Second Lieutenant Benjamin Eary

Associate Conductor

▾

Second Lieutenant Benjamin Eary serves as Flight Commander and Associate Conductor for the United States Air Force Band of the West. He assists in leading the Concert Band while supporting the organization's mission of honoring service, inspiring audiences, and representing the United States Air Force through musical excellence.

Lt Eary joined the Band of the West in 2024 after previous service as an enlisted Air Force musician and earning his commission through Officer Training School. His responsibilities include conducting performances, administrative leadership, and supporting outreach throughout the band's area of responsibility.
